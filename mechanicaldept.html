Mechanical Engineering (ME) Department

1. Department Overview

The Mechanical Engineering department provides students with a strong foundation in mechanical systems, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and material science. The department focuses on innovation and creativity in designing and building systems, making it one of the most versatile engineering disciplines.

2. Faculty Members

The faculty in the Mechanical Engineering department are experienced engineers and researchers specializing in areas such as robotics, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and automotive engineering.

3. Research Areas

  • Robotics
  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Manufacturing Processes
  • Automotive Engineering
  • Heat Transfer

4. Labs and Facilities

The department boasts state-of-the-art labs and workshops that provide hands-on experience in mechanical systems, thermodynamics, robotics, and automotive engineering. Students have access to modern tools and machines for their research and projects.

5. Career Opportunities

  • Mechanical Design Engineer
  • Automotive Engineer
  • Robotics Engineer
  • Thermal Systems Engineer
  • Manufacturing Engineer
  • Materials Engineer
