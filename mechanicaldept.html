DRK College
Department of Mechanical Engineering

Overview

The Department of Mechanical Engineering at DRK College is dedicated to providing top-tier technical education and research opportunities in the field of mechanical sciences. The curriculum is designed to foster innovation, sustainability, and the application of scientific principles to real-world problems.

Students are empowered to explore areas such as thermal engineering, fluid mechanics, materials science, robotics, and CAD/CAM systems through a combination of theory and hands-on projects.

Academic Objectives

Our goal is to cultivate mechanical engineers who possess strong analytical and design skills. The program emphasizes problem-solving, research, and teamwork to prepare students for diverse engineering roles across industries.

Courses are continually revised to meet industrial standards and prepare students for both technical careers and higher education pursuits.

Facilities & Infrastructure

The department features advanced laboratories in areas such as heat transfer, dynamics, manufacturing, and automation. Modern equipment and simulation software support practical training and research initiatives.

Collaborative spaces and workshops foster a learning environment where students can innovate and prototype engineering solutions.

Faculty

Experienced faculty members bring industry knowledge and academic excellence into the classroom. Their areas of expertise include energy systems, mechanical design, computational mechanics, and sustainable manufacturing.

Faculty actively participate in research, consultancy, and student mentoring, ensuring holistic development.

Career Opportunities

Graduates from the Mechanical Engineering department are equipped for careers in automotive, aerospace, manufacturing, energy, and research sectors. Many alumni have pursued advanced degrees and contributed to innovations in mechanical design and systems.

The department offers support through internships, career counseling, and recruitment drives in collaboration with leading engineering firms.
