B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Machine Learning)

Empowering Innovation through Machine Learning

1. Course Description

The B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Machine Learning) program focuses on imparting theoretical and practical knowledge of machine learning, pattern recognition, and predictive analytics. Students learn to develop systems that improve automatically through experience.

2. Duration

A 4-year full-time undergraduate program spread across 8 semesters, integrating core computer science fundamentals with specialized training in machine learning.

3. Subjects Covered

  • Foundations of Machine Learning
  • Supervised and Unsupervised Learning
  • Neural Networks and Deep Learning
  • Statistical Methods for Machine Learning
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Computer Vision
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Reinforcement Learning Techniques

4. Faculty Involved

The department features distinguished experts and researchers in machine learning, deep learning, big data technologies, and statistical analysis, ensuring a balance of academic excellence and industry relevance.

5. Career Opportunities

  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • AI Researcher
  • Business Intelligence Developer
  • Predictive Analytics Specialist
  • Computer Vision Engineer
  • Natural Language Processing Specialist
