Drk College - CSE-ML Program

B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering - Machine Learning (CSE-ML)

1. Course Description

The CSE-ML program offers advanced knowledge in the field of Machine Learning. The curriculum covers algorithm design, statistical learning, and real-time applications of ML in various domains such as healthcare, finance, and robotics.

2. Duration

This is a 4-year full-time undergraduate program divided into 8 semesters.

3. Subjects Covered

  • Introduction to Machine Learning
  • Supervised & Unsupervised Learning
  • Neural Networks
  • Statistical Learning
  • Data Mining
  • Computer Vision
  • AI & ML in Real-World Applications
  • Advanced Optimization Techniques

4. Faculty Involved

The faculty consists of leading experts in Machine Learning and AI, with a focus on both theoretical concepts and practical applications across diverse industries.

5. Career Opportunities

  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Data Analyst
  • AI Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • Research Scientist
  • Business Intelligence Developer
  • AI Application Developer
