B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

1. Course Description

The B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering program focuses on the study of electronic circuits, communications systems, and signal processing. Students will gain in-depth knowledge in both analog and digital electronics and apply this knowledge in various real-world applications.

2. Duration

This is a 4-year full-time undergraduate program divided into 8 semesters.

3. Subjects Covered

  • Circuit Theory & Design
  • Digital Electronics
  • Communication Systems
  • Signal Processing
  • Microprocessors & Microcontrollers
  • Electromagnetic Fields
