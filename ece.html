B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

Explore the future of technology and communication

1. Course Description

The B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering program focuses on designing, developing, and maintaining electronic devices and communication systems. It blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience in modern technologies like IoT, Embedded Systems, and Wireless Communication.

2. Duration

This is a 4-year full-time undergraduate program, divided into 8 semesters, structured to provide a deep understanding of core electronics and communication principles.

3. Subjects Covered

  • Electronic Devices and Circuits
  • Digital Electronics
  • Signals and Systems
  • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers
  • Communication Systems
  • VLSI Design
  • Embedded Systems
  • Internet of Things (IoT)

4. Faculty Involved

The department boasts experienced faculty members specializing in fields like VLSI, Communication Engineering, Embedded Systems, and Wireless Networks, committed to providing quality education and research opportunities.

5. Career Opportunities

  • Electronics Engineer
  • Communication Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Developer
  • IoT Solutions Architect
  • Network Engineer
  • VLSI Design Engineer
  • Research and Development Engineer
