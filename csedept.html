CSE Department

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department

1. Department Overview

The Computer Science and Engineering department is dedicated to providing students with the knowledge and skills to succeed in the rapidly growing technology industry. With a focus on modern computing systems, software development, and data-driven problem-solving, the department aims to foster innovation and creativity.

2. Faculty Members

Our faculty members are highly qualified professionals with expertise in a wide range of computer science domains, including software engineering, AI, machine learning, and cloud computing.

3. Research Areas

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Machine Learning (ML)
  • Cloud Computing
  • Data Science & Big Data
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Cybersecurity

4. Labs and Facilities

The department is equipped with state-of-the-art labs for software development, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and cloud computing. Students have access to modern workstations, software tools, and high-speed internet for optimal learning.

5. Career Opportunities

  • Software Developer
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • AI Researcher
  • Cloud Solutions Architect
  • Cybersecurity Specialist
