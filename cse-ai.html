B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence)

Shaping the Future with AI and Innovation

1. Course Description

The B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (Artificial Intelligence) program equips students with specialized knowledge in machine learning, deep learning, data science, and AI technologies. It focuses on building intelligent systems that can simulate human intelligence.

2. Duration

This is a 4-year full-time undergraduate program divided into 8 semesters, offering an in-depth curriculum in computer science along with specialization in Artificial Intelligence.

3. Subjects Covered

  • Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine Learning Algorithms
  • Deep Learning
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Robotics and Automation
  • Data Science and Big Data Analytics
  • Computer Vision
  • Reinforcement Learning

4. Faculty Involved

The department includes distinguished professors and researchers specializing in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Cognitive Computing, actively involved in cutting-edge research and industry collaborations.

5. Career Opportunities

  • Artificial Intelligence Engineer
  • Machine Learning Scientist
  • Data Scientist
  • Research Scientist (AI/ML)
  • Computer Vision Engineer
  • Natural Language Processing Engineer
  • Robotics Engineer
